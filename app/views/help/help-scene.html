Better BAC Help

Each scene has it's own help page, so if you need help on a particular screen, go to that screen and choose "Help" from the app menu.

Can't find the help you're looking for? Have a suggestion for this app? Let me know by emailing me.

Main Help

The main screen will show you four things:

  • Your current estimated BAC
  • The estimated time (in minutes) until your bac would be at zero
  • The estimated time (in minutes) until your BAC would be at the legal limit that you set in the preferences screen (default = 0.08, which is the limit in all US states)
  • A list of the drinks you have entered

When open, the app will automatically update your status every minute. You may close the app, and it will catch up the next time you open it.

As time passes, and your BAC decreases, the app will decrease the BAC of the oldest "current" drink in your list until it reaches zero. When a drink in your list reaches zero, it is considered "out of your system" and will be moved to the "History" section of the list.

You can delete a drink by swiping it. When you delete a drink, it will be as if you had never entered that drink.

The drink list

Each drink in the list shows the following:

  • Drink name (top left)
  • Drink vol. and abv (bottom left)
  • The time the drink was entered (top right)
  • The current BAC as a fraction of the total BAC contributed by that drink (bottom right)

Adding a drink

When you have a drink, register it by clicking the "Add" button at the bottom of the page. This will take you to a scene that allows you to choose a drink from a list or enter a custom drink.

Have another

If you tap a drink in your drinks list, you will be taken to the custom drink page with all the fields pre-filled based on the drink you tapped.
