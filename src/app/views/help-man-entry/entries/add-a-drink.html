Adding a preset drink

When you have a drink, register it by clicking the "Add" button at the bottom of the page. This will take you to a scene that allows you to choose a drink from a list or enter a custom drink.

Adding a custom drink

To add a custom drink, tap "Add" from the main screen, and then tap "Custom drink".

This scene allows you to describe a drink you have consumed so that it can be incorporated into your BAC calculation.

The Alcohol By Volume field is the percentage of the drink that is pure alcohol. Enter a number only--do not include the % sign. More information about finding a drink's ABV can be found in the "How do I know the ABV of my drink?" section of the User Manual.

The volume field should be ounces if you are using imperial units or milliliters if you are using metric units. A typical bottled beer is 12 oz. or 355ml. A 750ml bottle of Wine is about 25 oz. A glass of wine is usually between 3 and 6 oz. (89-177ml). A standard "shot" in the U.S. is 1.5oz.

The time field may be used if you had a drink a while ago, but forgot to enter it at the time. You may only enter a time going back 24 hours. If you enter a "future" time (e.g. it is 7:00pm and you enter 8:00pm) then the drink will be interpreted as being from the previous day rather than later on in the current day. So in our example, that would be 8:00pm last night rather than 8:00pm later tonight.

I suspect that if you crossed time zones between drinks, it would screw up your calculations, but I have neither the means nor the ambition to test this.

You may use the "back" gesture to leave this scene and return to the main status screen without entering a new drink.

Have another

If you tap a drink in your drinks list, you will be taken to the custom drink page with all the fields pre-filled based on the drink you tapped. This creates a new duplicate drink. It does not edit the drink you tapped.
