The main screen will show you four things:

  • Your current estimated BAC
  • The estimated time (in minutes) until your bac would be at zero
  • The estimated time (in minutes) until your BAC would be at the limit that you set in the preferences screen
  • A list of the drinks you have entered

When open, the app will automatically update your status every 30 seconds. You may close the app, and it will catch up the next time you open it.

As time passes, and your BAC decreases, the app will decrease the BAC of the oldest drink in your system until it reaches zero. When a drink in your list reaches zero, it is considered "out of your system".

You can delete a drink by swiping it. When you delete a drink, it will be as if you had never entered that drink.

Each drink in the list shows the following:

  • Drink name (first row)
  • Drink vol. / abv / B.A.C. added by this drink (second row left)
  • The time the drink was entered (second row right)
  • Your B.A.C. before and after having this drink (third row)
