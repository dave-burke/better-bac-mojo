To view your BAC graph, tap the "Graph" button on the main scene

This scene displays a graph of your BAC over time. The default view shows you all the drinks you've had in the last 12 hours.

You can tap the box labeled "Graph Time Period" to select a "24 hour" view of your BAC over the last 24 hours or "All" to see a graph of your entire history.

Note that the graph is not updated automatically like the main status screen is.
