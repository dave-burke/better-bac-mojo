Main Scene

The main screen will show you four things:

  • Your current estimated BAC
  • The estimated time (in minutes) until your bac would be at zero
  • The estimated time (in minutes) until your BAC would be at the legal limit that you set in the preferences screen (default = 0.08, which is the limit in all US states)
  • A list of the drinks you have entered

When open, the app will automatically update your status every 30 seconds. You may close the app, and it will catch up the next time you open it.

As time passes, and your BAC decreases, the app will decrease the BAC of the oldest drink in your list until it reaches zero. When a drink in your list reaches zero, it is considered "out of your system".

You can delete a drink by swiping it. When you delete a drink, it will be as if you had never entered that drink.

The drink list

Each drink in the list shows the following:

  • Drink name (first row)
  • Drink vol. / abv / B.A.C. added by this drink (second row left)
  • The time the drink was entered (second row right)
  • Your B.A.C. before and after having this drink (third row)

Adding a drink

When you have a drink, register it by clicking the "Add" button at the bottom of the page. This will take you to a scene that allows you to choose a drink from a list or enter a custom drink.

Have another

If you tap a drink in your drinks list, you will be taken to the custom drink page with all the fields pre-filled based on the drink you tapped. This creates a new duplicate drink. It does not edit the drink you tapped.
